# Capgemini Invent NA — ExCo Branding & Design Reference

Extracted from the official Templates folder: `Invent NA slide template.pptx` and `Capgemini_Group-Presentation_September-25 1.pptx`.

---

## Slide Dimensions

- **16:9 widescreen** — 13.33" × 7.5" (33.87cm × 19.05cm)
- Both templates are identical on this.

---

## Fonts

**Ubuntu exclusively. No substitutions.**

| Role | Variant |
|------|---------|
| Headings / Titles | Ubuntu Medium |
| Body text | Ubuntu Regular |
| Light/supporting | Ubuntu Light |

Ubuntu is a free Google font — must be installed to render correctly. Fallbacks (Arial, Verdana) are non-brand and should never appear in final deliverables.

## Slide Layout & Margins

### Content Grid

| Zone | Position |
|------|----------|
| Left content margin | 0.60" from left |
| Right content edge | 12.73" (0.60" right margin) |
| Eyebrow/subtitle top | y ≈ 0.46" |
| Slide title top | y ≈ 0.76" |
| Body content starts | y ≈ 1.5–1.8" |
| Source citation strip | y = 6.89" |
| Footer strip | y = 7.27" |

### Header Structure (Standard Content Slide)

```
[0.01", 0.00"]  "Capgemini Invent" wordmark text (top-left)
[12.11", 0.21"] Capgemini logo PNG, 0.99"×0.32" (top-right)
[0.60", 0.46"]  Eyebrow / subtitle label (14pt light)
[0.60", 0.76"]  SLIDE TITLE (24pt Ubuntu Medium)
```

### Footer Structure (All Slides)

```
[left]    Project / deck name (8pt)
[center-right]  "Company Sensitive" (8pt)  ← NOT "Confidential" for Invent NA internal
[right]   "© Capgemini 2026. All rights reserved." (8pt)
[far right] Slide number ‹#› (8pt)
```

## Logo Placement

- **Top-left wordmark:** "Capgemini Invent" text at `(0.01", 0.00")`, size `2.01"×0.28"` — inherited from master, do not move
- **Top-right logo:** Capgemini PNG at `(12.11", 0.21")`, size `0.99"×0.32"` — do not move
- **Logo marks:** Two colored freeform shapes (`#12ABDB` + `#0070AD`) at `(12.73", 0.37")` in some masters

---

## Recurring Design Motifs

**Dot/grid pattern:** Coral (`#FF816E`) rectangles (~0.91"×0.60" each) arranged in a 5-row × 12-column matrix across the upper master area. Appears as subtle texture on dark backgrounds.

**Horizontal accent bar:** Thin bar (`~0.197"` tall) in turquoise, electric blue, or the 4-color gradient ribbon, placed at the header/body boundary.

**Section dividers:** Section number at lower-left (large display type), section title right-side at 48pt white, on dark navy or photo background.

**"Make it real."** tagline appears on all end slides.

## Quick Rules — Dark vs. Light Slides

| Element | Dark Slide | Light Slide |
|---------|-----------|-------------|

| Secondary accent | `#00D5D0` | `#00828E` |
| Tertiary accent | `#FEB100` | `#BE4D01` |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Correct Approach |
|---------|-----------------|
| Using "Company Confidential" on Invent NA decks | Use "Company **Sensitive**" |
| Substituting Arial for Ubuntu | Install Ubuntu; never substitute |
| Moving logos from their fixed positions | Logos inherit from master — do not override |
| Using column headers in black | Column headers are always `#12ABDB` in Invent NA layouts |
| Copyright year wrong | Invent NA = **2026**; Group template = 2025 |
| Using dark-only accent colors on light slides | Switch to the "on Light" variants (Teal, Burnt Orange, Maroon, Mauve) |
